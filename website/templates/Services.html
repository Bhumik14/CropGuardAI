Our Services

Services We Offer

We And Our Services Are Available For You Anytime.

Real-Time Disease Detection and Alerts

This service offers real-time monitoring and detection of crop diseases using AI-driven tools. Farmers can upload images or input specific data about their crops (e.g., weather conditions, soil health), and the system analyzes the information to detect early signs of disease.

Drag and Drop Disease Identification Tool

This service allows farmers to easily upload images of crops showing signs of disease by simply dragging and dropping the images into the designated area on the website. The AI system then analyzes the images in real-time, identifying the type of disease and suggesting potential treatments or preventive measures.

Weather App

The weather app would provide real-time, localized weather data and forecasts tailored to specific agricultural needs. By integrating weather information with the crop disease prediction system, the app can offer insights into how upcoming weather conditions might influence disease risks, irrigation needs, and overall crop health.
